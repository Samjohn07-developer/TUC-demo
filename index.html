About the Association

This platform brings together students from Tana institutions to promote unity, development, and engagement.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered youth driving inclusive, sustainable, and transformative change in their communities and beyond.

Our Mission

To engage, equip, and elevate young people as active leaders and partners in governance, innovation, and social justice through advocacy, capacity building, and strategic partnerships.

Our Purpose

To bridge the gap between youth potential and decision-making power by creating pathways for authentic representation, meaningful engagement, and systemic influence at county, national, and global levels.
